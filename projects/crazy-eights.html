Crazy Eights

Completed:

12/15/2024

Completion Time:

4 weeks

Roles on Project:

Sole Designer, Developer, and Artist.

Description:

This is a passion project I made before apply to graduate school. I made all the art, code, and designed the game. It isn't "completed," but hasn't been worked on in favor of other projects. Additional action items for this project are changing the sprites of enemy cards so you can't see what they're playing, implementing multiple enemies, adding animations and sounds, adding complexity to the enemy AI, and creating a mechanic where players and enemies can "cheat."
