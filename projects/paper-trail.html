Paper Trail

Completed:

07/25/2025

Completion Time:

2 Weeks.

Roles on Project:

Producer, Programmer, Source Code Control Manager, and SFX Curator.

Description:

My second year at Girls Make Games as a counselor, I led a team of two 11 and two 14 year-old girls in making a horror puzzle game in Unity. I taught the girls for one week, then led the team in making Paper Trail for the last two weeks of camp. The game won the Demo Day competition, and will be developed and published on Steam. This game was also showcased at the Game of the Year Awards, when Girls Make Games won the TGA Game Changer Award.
