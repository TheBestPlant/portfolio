Pizza Saucer

Completed:

12/10/2025

Completion Time:

13 Weeks.

Roles on Project:

Gameplay Programemr, Narrative Designer, and Audio Curator.

Description:

My first project made in Graduate School at Rochester Institute of Technology. Made in 13 weeks in a team of five, three of which were programmers, I implemented the movement of aliens, grabbing pizza toppings, and fixed bugs. Additionally, I wrote over 1500 lines of dialogue, and created topping "interactions." I also sourced or created many of the game's sound effects.
